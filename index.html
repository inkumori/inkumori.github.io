X-DRUMS

Where is the best place to record an album? Los Angeles Music Studio!

Help a Japanese Drummer get to Los Angeles! You’ll need to jump over drumkits, step on hi-hats and drumsticks to pick up all the notes on my way. You need to find an aircraft to leave Okinawa Island. Beware of all the dangers and keep in mind that speed matters in order to reach the top of the leaderboard.

Play with us and have Fun! Banzai!

8-bit pixel art platformer game. Inspired by Yoshiki and X-Japan.
